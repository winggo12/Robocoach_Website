In the first month, we collected the dataset about golf actions – cameras in different angles

For the whole complete action of a golf swing, we divide it into different stages, including Backswing, Downswing, Standing (Ready Position) and Final (Follow-Through).


Around 300 videos are taken

Each one with the length of around 10 seconds


All we need is to obtain the human key point datasets


Using Neural Network to Classify the Problems of a Golf Swing

Provide videos of false golf swing for neural network to learn (No turning, Moving your feet , etc)

Result : Accuracy is around 70% and the real-time performance is not too good
